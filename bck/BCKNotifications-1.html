NOTIFICATION NO. 01/09

Whereas, the Disciplinary Committee of the Bar Council of Kerala by notification No.4/2008 dated 16.06.2008 notified for general information that SHRI.C.G.VASUDEVAN NAIR, Advocate, Valiyathuruthiyil House, Ranni, Pathanamthitta enrolled with the Bar Council of Kerala  with Roll No.K/36/1961 on 13.3.1961  has been SUSPENDED from practicing as an Advocate for a period of 5 years as per the order of the Disciplinary Committee in case No.22/07 and whereas, the Bar Council of India vide order dated 15.07.2008 stayed the operation of the impugned order under Appeal No.44/2008, it is hereby further notified that the operation of the above  said order in DC Case No.22/2007 is stayed till next date of hearing of appeal No.44/2008.

DISCIPLINARY COMMITTEE

 KBC/DC  V/Cir./         /09                                                                       23/01/2009

 NOTIFICATION NO. 02/09

It is hereby notified for general information  that the Disciplinary Committee of the Bar Council of India vide its order dated 17.12.2008 in BCI TR Case No.33/2000 SUSPENDED from practice  of the Advocate SRI.K.P.ARAVINDAKSHAN, (Edasseril Bldg., Market Road, Cochin 35, who was enrolled as an advocate on 11.08.1973, Roll No.K/38A/1973) for a period of one year from 09.01.2009.  Further it is ordered that the Advocate Sri.K.P.Aravindakshan  must pay the  correct share of compensation amount of the complainant with interest @ 9% p.a from the date of receipt of the amount till the same is repaid with cost of Rs.10,000/- within one month from 09.01.2009; and in default, he will be further suspended from practice as an advocate for a further period of 6 months to  run consecutively.

This is for your information.
